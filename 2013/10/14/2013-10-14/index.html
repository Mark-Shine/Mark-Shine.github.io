创建多个ssh id for Mac OSX, GitBash, and Linux

因为业务需要，不得不考虑用多个id连接服务器，在此考虑这个教程

1. ssh-keygen -f ~/.ssh/personalid -C "personalid
2. vim ~/.ssh/config
3. Host workdid
   HostName bitbucket.org
   IdentityFile ~/.ssh/workdid
   Host personalid
   HostName bitbucket.org
   IdentityFile ~/.ssh/personalid

完毕
